OUR MISSION

disABLEDperson, Inc. is a public charity organization since 2002 whose primary focus is disability employment. We want to help you, a person with a disability find employment. So come, post your resume and look through our job listings. Our site also offers both email and SMS texting job alerts! Sign up and get job listings sent to you every day. Even though our sites are nationally based, you can search for local job listings by simply using our advanced job search functionality. For example, you can do a search for “Accounting Chicago”. All the accounting job listings in Chicago will show up for you. Our organization’s mission is to increase the rate of disability employment for our community members. “Help me find a job” is a phrase that many in our community ask us daily. They want to know that if they conduct a job search on our job search website that it will help them in finding a job.

WHAT WE DO AT SAHYOG

Do the right thing

 We’re not here to bash other disability job boards. We’re glad they exist, and we will always support any effort to help people overcoming these daily challenges find careers in which they are fulfilled. We are different, though, and we want everyone to be aware of that. We're committed to helping our employers achieve compliance with relevant statutes and policies, all while doing the right thing.

We’re Not Here for Profit

 Finally, we want everyone to understand that even though we’ve earned a prominent place relative to the rest of the disability job boards market, we’re not here to maximize our profit. In fact, we’re not even a for-profit entity. We are a 501(c)(3) charitable organization whose mission is to reduce the high unemployment rate of individuals and veterans with disabilities. We are here to serve, and that’s that

We’re Also Doing It Right

 DWe’re proud of our place among the disability job boards market. We attract thousands of unique visitors to our Web site every single day, and these are people both looking for work and looking for talent. We have a long and successful track record of helping individuals and employers find matches, and that’s why so many of our employers keep coming back to us when it’s time for more hiring
