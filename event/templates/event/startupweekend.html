PITCHERS
"The secret of selling great products is to sell the idea of the products before you actually start selling it." Pitchers is an annual event organized by EDC, TIET to promote and nurture the art of pitching ideas among the budding Entrepreneurs. This event not only promotes the basic startup qualities like Pitching and Project management but also enhances leadership and team work. It also gives an idea of the competitive environment of the startup Ecosystem.

START UP EXPO
“ A lot of people have ideas, but there are few who decide to do something about them” We at EDC, TIET are always on the lookout for the ‘Next Big Thing’. This is why we started StartUp Expo, a global platform where some of the best upcoming startups can get an opportunity to pitch their ideas in front of investors and VCs. Last year, we partnered with The Punjab Chamber of Commerce and Industry. With over 50 registrations, 8 startups were selected and it was held at The Lalit, Chandigarh on 5th November, 2019.
