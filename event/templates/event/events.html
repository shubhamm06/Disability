E-TALKS

26 APRIL - 21 JUNE, 2020

In order to replace the quarantine boredom with a tête-à-tête with some top players in the entrepreneurial world,EDC came up with E -TALKS-A series of interactive sessions with entrepreneurs

E-TALKS

The Webinar Series involved a total of 10 sessions from 26 April to 21 June. Each webinar included an hour-long informative session by a business professional along with a question-answer round towards the end of the Webinar Series, each session welcomed more than 90 participants including by TIET Students, Students from outside TIET, and TIET faculty. The speakers at E-Talks included personalities like Mr. Samar Singla, CEO/Founder at Jugnoo; Mr. Ankit Prasad, Founder at Bobble.AI and, Mr. Sudeep Gupta, Director of International Business at OYO Rooms and many more prominent business person. The Webinar Series was a success with something unique to learn at every session.

VL-WEEKEND

MARCH, 2021

Venture Lab,a 3 day workshop instilling and promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among the participants by empowering today's youth with the tools needed to become the initiator of change towards a self sustainable future.

VL-WEEKEND

The workshop expects a heavy footfall courtesy of students of Thapar Institute and other well known colleges of North India. The 3 day workshop will help the students understand how to execute an idea about something and make a profitable, marketable, feasible business out of it in the competitive environment of today. Participants will be taught things like how to pitch their ideas, value proposition, the benefits of collaboration and the art of sustaining it in high-pressure situations and many more things which are essential for a startup.
